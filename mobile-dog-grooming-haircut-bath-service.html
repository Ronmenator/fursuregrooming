Full-Service Mobile Dog Grooming with Haircut & Bath

Professional grooming brought straight to your driveway


Our full-service grooming package is designed for dogs who need more than a simple bath. Whether it’s time for a seasonal haircut, breed-standard grooming, or a total refresh, our mobile dog groomers will deliver personalized care from nose to tail — without the stress of a salon visit.

What's Included in the Full-Service Haircut & Bath Package?
  • Breed-appropriate full body haircut
  • Warm bath with coat-specific shampoo and conditioning treatment
  • Fluff blow-dry with brush
  • Dog nail trimming with Dremel
  • Gentle ear cleaning
  • Teeth and breath refresh
  • Paw pad shave out and sanitary area tidy up
  • Finishing touch: bandana, bow, or spritz of dog-friendly perfume

Who Is This Service Best For?

  • Dogs with coats that require regular trims or shaving
  • Doodles, poodles, schnauzers, spaniels, and more
  • Long-haired breeds needing style upkeep
  • Pet parents seeking consistent, professional grooming results

What Makes Our Full Groom Special?

We don't rush through cuts. Each groom includes a consultation, so we understand your dog's coat, health needs, and preferences. We aim for comfort, safety, and style every time. Want a teddy bear cut, summer shave, or breed-specific look? Just let us know.

Why Choose Mobile Grooming?

As a trusted mobile dog groomer in the North Denver area, we bring professional care right to your doorstep — no driving or waiting rooms required. Our grooming van is fully equipped for every service your dog needs — we just need a driveway or safe parking spot. It's ideal for busy families, dogs with anxiety, senior pets, or anyone looking for a more relaxed grooming experience.
