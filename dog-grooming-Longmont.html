Our Longmont Grooming Services

We tailor our services to your pup's breed, age, and personality.

Full-Service Grooming Package:

Includes bath, brushing, full-body haircut, nail trim, ear cleaning, and teeth & breath refresh.

Full Bath Package:

Our bath packages includes a bath service with nail trimming, ear cleaning, teeth & breath refresh. You can add an additional sanitary trim service.

Nail Trim & Refresh:

Nail trimming with Dremel, teeth cleaning, and breath refresh.

Deshedding Treatment:

Bath with deshedding shampoo & conditioner, followed by a thorough brush-out to reduce shedding.

Puppy Grooming:

Gentle introduction to grooming with a warm bath, light brushing, nail trim, and lots of TLC.
