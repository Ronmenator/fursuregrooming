About Us

Welcome to Fur Sure Grooming —your local, family-owned mobile dog grooming service based in Westminster, Colorado! We proudly serve the surrounding communities of Denver, Arvada, Northglenn, Broomfield, and nearby areas with professional, stress-free grooming right at your doorstep.

As lifelong pet lovers and experienced dog groomers, we know how important it is to provide personalized care, gentle handling, and one-on-one attention. That’s why we offer cage-free, mobile dog grooming, in a fully equipped van - right outside your home.

We make grooming convenient and comforting for both pets and their humans. From baths and brushing to nail trimming, deshedding, and full-body haircuts, our groomers tailor each grooming service to your dog’s needs. Best of all? There are no extra call-out fees! At Fur Sure, we’re committed to making pet grooming easy, loving, and full of tail wags—because your dog deserves nothing less.

Built on Love & Trust

At Fur Sure Grooming, we’re more than just a mobile pet service — we’re a small, family-run business rooted in compassion, trust, and a genuine love for dogs. When you book with us, you’re supporting real people who treat every dog like family.

We started this journey with our own rescue pup, Mila. She came to us scared and uncertain, and through gentle care and patience, we helped her blossom. Mila is now the heart of Fur Sure Grooming—and the inspiration behind our commitment to gentle, stress-free grooming for every dog we meet.

Whether your dog is shy, energetic, or needs a little extra TLC, our team is here to help them feel safe, calm, and cared for. Explore our full list of grooming services and see how we can pamper your pup with love and expertise.

When you choose Fur Sure, you’re not just getting convenience — you’re choosing a grooming experience full of kindness, safety, and heart. And we wouldn’t have it any other way.
