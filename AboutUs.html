Furry Important Questions

How does Fur Sure Mobile services work?

We make grooming easy and stress-free—with no call-out service fees! Your groomer arrives right on time (we'll text if there's any delay), greets your pup at your door, chats with you about any special needs, and gets started—all from the comfort of your driveway. Your dog never leaves home! 
Grooms typically take 1 to 2.5 hours, depending on breed, size, and fluff level. Once your pup is fresh and fabulous, they're returned right back to you—with a quick grooming recap just for you!

Do you groom large dogs too?

Yes, we groom big dogs—but for now, we normally cap it at 75 lb. We occasionally accept larger dogs on a case by case basis - reach out! However, we're actively working on expanding our setup to accommodate larger pups, so stay tuned!

What products do you use?

We use all-natural and pet-friendly products that are used by the best groomers in the industry, free of detergents and safe for your pet. Our Mobile Salon is outfitted with top of the range equipment and use warm water during grooming service to ensure you pup enjoy a luxury spa treatment.

Do my pets need to be vaccinated?

Yes! For everyone's safety (including ours), a valid rabies certificate must be on file before grooming—because we prefer cuddles over close calls!

Can I watch while you groom my dog?

We'd love to show you inside our van, but for your pup's safety (and our insurance's sanity), we have to say no.

Do you offer recurring appointments?

Yes, we offer recurring appointments to keep your pup looking fresh and fabulous! Regular grooming means less mess, fewer tangles, and a happier pooch. We recommend booking your next appointment before we leave.

Bite-Sized Tips for Dog Parents

Regular Grooming

Regular grooming isn't just about keeping your pup looking cute — it's essential for their health. Brushing removes dirt, reduces shedding, and keeps skin healthy. Nail trims prevent discomfort and joint issues. A well-groomed dog is a happy, healthy dog!

4-Week Rule

Keeping your dog on a 4-week grooming schedule helps prevent matting, odors, and buildup of dirt and oils. It also keeps nails trimmed before they get too long. Routine grooming catches potential health issues early. Plus, your dog stays fresh and fabulous all month long!

Clean Home

Regular grooming doesn't just benefit your dog — it keeps your home cleaner too. Less shedding, less dander, and fewer muddy paw prints on your floors and furniture. A well-groomed pup means fewer messes to clean up. Your dog stays fresh, and so does your house!

Mobile Grooming

Mobile grooming means no stressful car rides, no waiting rooms, and no cages. Your pup gets one-on-one attention right at your doorstep. It's perfect for anxious, elderly, or busy-bee pets. Fur Sure Grooming service North Denver area. Fast, convenient, and stress-free — what's not to love?

Personally Invested

With Fur Sure mobile grooming, you're supporting a local small business in North Denver that truly cares about your pet. We remember your dog's quirks, favorite treats, and grooming preferences. You're not just another appointment — you're part of our pack. Fur sure, that makes a difference!

Mental Wellness

Grooming isn't just physical — it's mentally enriching for dogs. The routine, touch, and attention provide stimulation and reduce anxiety. Dogs feel more comfortable and confident when they're clean and well cared for. It's like a spa day and therapy session rolled into one!
