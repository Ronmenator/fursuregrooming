About Us

Welcome to Fur Sure Grooming — a family-run business inspired by our love for dogs and a passion for gentle, personalized care. Based in Westminster, Colorado, we're proud to be part of a close-knit community of pet lovers who believe that grooming should feel safe, calm, and full of heart.

Our story begins with Mila, our rescue pup who taught us the power of patience and trust. When she first came into our lives, she was timid and unsure. With time, kindness, and a lot of love, Mila transformed — and she's been the heart of Fur Sure Grooming ever since. Every service we offer carries her spirit: warm, gentle, and deeply personal.

We specialize in building trust with each dog we work with. Whether your pup is shy, excitable, elderly, or simply needs a little extra TLC, we meet them where they are — always with empathy, patience, and individualized attention. Our team provides focused, one-on-one care in a calm setting designed to reduce stress and help your dog feel comfortable and safe from start to finish.

Built on Love & Trust

We're not just groomers — we're neighbors, animal lovers, and trusted partners in your dog's wellbeing. And while we primarily work with families in Westminster and nearby communities, what matters most is not where we go, but how we care.

Whether your dog is shy, energetic, or needs a little extra TLC, our team is here to help them feel safe, calm, and cared for. Explore our full list of grooming services and see how we can pamper your pup with love and expertise. Unsure and have questions? Contact us — we'd love to hear from you.

When you choose Fur Sure, you're choosing more than convenience. You're joining a circle of care built on kindness, trust, and a lot of tail wags. And we wouldn't have it any other way.
