Grooming Policy

Required Vaccinations

Rabies Vaccination is required and should be up to date. Our office requires a copy of the rabies certificate on file.

Matting, Shedding & Coat Issues

A 15 minute brush out is included in the initial grooming package. When extra brushing and de-shedding beyond normal maintenance incur an additional fee of $2/minute, up to 20 minutes will be charged if the de-matting is deemed humane. For the safety of your dog we do NOT brush for LONGER THAN 15 minutes in one service. These fees will be made aware to the owner at drop off.

For excessive matting or matting close to the skin, shaving down the coat is the only option. If a pre-shave is needed before the bath, a $50-$70 fee applies (depending on weight) due to tool wear and extra time. Fur Sure grooming has the right to refuse a service that will result in inhumane grooming for the dog.

We do not shave double-coated breeds (e.g., Huskies, German Shepherds) due to health risks. We  recommend a regular grooming schedule.

Fleas, Skin Diseases, & Injuries

- If fleas or parasites are found on your dog, a flea bath is required. We will make every attempt to notify the parent, but if we cannot, a flea shampoo will be automatically added to the services of $10-$20 depending on weight. We value the safety of the pets in our care, so if a customer chooses to refuse a flea shampoo, we will need to stop the groom and send the dog home. A customer will only be charged for services that were completed and may be charged a salon sanitation fee of $50.

- If a dog has parasites or other contagious diseases, depending on the severity, the Fur Sure groomer has the right to refuse service until a vet has cleared the pet for grooming. If parasites are found during the groom, the customer will be contacted immediately and be made aware of the $50 sanitation fee. 

- To ensure safety for all pets, we DO NOT service pets with ring worm. If ring worm were to be found on a pet while grooming. The groom will be stopped IMMEDIATELY and the customer will be charged a $50 sanitation fee.

Depending on the severity, your dog may be required to see a veterinarian for treatment before grooming services are rendered.

Cancellations

Please provide at least 48 hours' notice for reschedules or cancellations. Last-minute changes impact our business, so a $45 fee applies for cancellations or reschedules with less than 24 hours' notice. Frequent cancellations and/or no-shows may result in a non-refundable 50% deposit OR pre-payment being required to book future appointments.
If you're running late, we can wait up to 15 minutes. After that, we may have to do a partial groom or, if too late, charge a no-show fee due to scheduling disruptions. 

Special Handling

If your pup needs extra care due to age, disability, or behavior, please let us know in advance. A handling fee of $30 may apply if additional time is needed. For safety reasons, Fur Sure Grooming reserve the right to stop the groom if we feel that either the groomer or your pup is at risk.

Severe Matting Disclaimer

Severe matting can hide underlying skin issues that may only be revealed once the coat is shaved. Common findings include bruising, scarring, wounds, parasites, infections, redness, swelling, and bald spots where hair has been pulled from the follicle. Fur Sure Grooming is not responsible for any pre-existing conditions or any effects caused by matting or its removal.

Allowing mats or pelts to form, as well as the shaving process, can pose health risks such as nicks, cuts, bruising, skin irritation, dermatitis, hair loss or texture changes, joint or muscle tenderness, and more.
Any veterinary care needed due to matting, pelting, or their removal is solely the owner's responsibility.
