Grooming Policy

Required Vaccinations

Rabies vaccination is required by Colorado law and must be kept up to date. Our office also requires a current copy of the rabies certificate on file.

Special Handling

If your pup needs extra care due to age, disability, or behavior, please let us know in advance. A handling fee of $30 may apply if additional time is needed. For safety reasons, Fur Sure Grooming reserve the right to stop the groom if we feel that either the groomer or your pup is at risk.

Matting, Shedding & Coat Issues

A 15-minute brush-out is included with each initial grooming package. If additional brushing or de-shedding beyond this standard maintenance is needed, an extra fee of $2 per minute will apply. We may continue up to 20 minutes if the process is deemed humane, but for your pet's safety, we do not brush for more than 15 minutes in any single session. Any extra fees will be disclosed at drop-off.

Please note that "de-matting" or complete coat removal can significantly alter your pet's appearance and may reveal pre-existing skin or health issues, for which Fur Sure Grooming Co. cannot be held liable. In cases of severe matting or matting close to the skin, shaving down the coat may be the only humane option. If a pre-shave is needed before bathing, an additional fee of $50–$70 (depending on weight) applies, due to extra time and tool wear. Fur Sure Grooming Co. reserves the right to refuse any service that would be inhumane for your dog.

We also do not shave double-coated breeds (e.g., Huskies, German Shepherds) because of associated health risks. Instead, we recommend a regular grooming schedule to help keep your pet's coat healthy.

Severe matting can hide problems like bruising, scarring, wounds, parasites, infections, redness, swelling, or bald spots, which may become apparent after shaving. Fur Sure Grooming Co. is not responsible for any pre-existing conditions or the effects of matting or its removal. Allowing mats to form—or shaving them off—may lead to nicks, cuts, bruising, skin irritation, dermatitis, hair loss or texture changes, and joint or muscle tenderness. Any required veterinary care is solely the owner's responsibility.

Fleas & Skin Diseases

If fleas or parasites are discovered on your dog, a flea bath is required. We will make every attempt to notify the parent, but if we cannot reach you, a flea shampoo ($10–$20 based on weight) will be automatically added. For the safety of all pets in our care, if you refuse a flea bath, we must stop the groom and send the dog home. You may also be charged a $50 salon sanitation fee.

If your dog has parasites or a contagious disease, we reserve the right to refuse service until cleared by a veterinarian. Should parasites be found mid-groom, you will be contacted immediately regarding the required $50 salon sanitation fee. 

We do not service pets with ringworm. If ringworm is identified during grooming, we will stop immediately, send the dog home, and apply a $50 sanitation fee.

Depending on severity, your dog may need veterinary treatment before grooming services can resume. If a grooming must be stopped due to any of these issues, you will only be charged for completed services plus any applicable fees.

Cancellations

We follow Birch Elementary School's inclement weather closures. We do our best to remain open as long as conditions are safe. If you're unsure about your appointment, please call or text us to confirm.

Cancellations or rescheduling require at least 48 hours' notice to avoid the full appointment fee. In cases of inclement weather, family emergencies, or other uncontrollable circumstances, the groomer may waive this fee within the 48-hour window. Frequent cancellations or no-shows may lead to a non-refundable 50% deposit or pre-payment for future bookings.

If you are running late, we can accommodate up to 15 minutes. Beyond that, we may need to provide a partial groom or—if excessively late—charge a no-show fee due to scheduling disruptions. Thank you for your understanding.

We're offer convenient text reminders and updates from Fur Sure Grooming. By requesting an appointment, you consent to receive text messages at the number you provide, including reminders, schedule changes, and important updates. Your number will be used only for this purpose and not shared. Message and data rates may apply; frequency may vary. Reply STOP to end messages or HELP for more information.
